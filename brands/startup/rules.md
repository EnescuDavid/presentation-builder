# Brand Rules: Startup

## Mandatory Slides
- Title slide with bold, engaging headline
- Contact/CTA slide at the end

## Tone & Voice
- Casual and approachable -- first-person plural ("Wir") encouraged
- Question-style titles spark curiosity ("Was waere, wenn...?")
- Emoji allowed sparingly in slide content (not in titles)
- Narrative bullet style -- tell a story, don't just list facts

## Visual Style
- Image-heavy -- prefer full-bleed images and card grids over dense tables
- Bold colors and high contrast encouraged
- Generous animation -- use entrance animations liberally
- Quote slides for customer testimonials are high-value

## Chart & Data Conventions
- Simple charts preferred (bar, doughnut) over complex ones (waterfall, harvey-balls)
- Round numbers, no decimal precision unless critical
- Conversational data labels ("2x schneller", not "+100%")

## Prohibited Patterns
- Avoid data-table and harvey-balls -- too formal for startup audiences
- No dense multi-column tables

## Corporate Compliance
- Logo in top-right corner
- "Internal" confidentiality footer
