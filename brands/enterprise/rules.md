# Brand Rules: Enterprise

## Mandatory Slides
- Title slide with formal statement headline
- Agenda slide for all presentations
- Summary/takeaway slide before contact slide
- Contact slide with full corporate details

## Tone & Voice
- High formality at all times -- no colloquial language
- Statement-style titles that assert a conclusion ("Umsatz stieg um 15%")
- Verb-first German action titles in formal register
- No English words mixed into German content
- No emoji under any circumstances

## Visual Style
- Data-heavy -- prefer frameworks, metrics, data-tables, and comparison layouts
- Conservative animation -- minimal entrance effects, no playful transitions
- Whitespace for gravitas, not density
- Avoid image-full-bleed and card-grid -- too informal for enterprise context

## Chart & Data Conventions
- Precise numbers with units and sources
- Use --color-primary for highlight data series
- Multi-series charts use the brand color palette in order
- Every data slide must cite its source
- Harvey-balls for maturity assessments where applicable

## Prohibited Patterns
- No emoji in any context
- No image-full-bleed slides
- No card-grid layouts
- No casual language or informal abbreviations
- No quote slides (reserved for internal executive communications only)

## Corporate Compliance
- "Strictly Confidential" on every slide footer
- Logo top-left on all slides
- Date format: DD.MM.YYYY
- Company name in footer: "Enterprise Corp."
